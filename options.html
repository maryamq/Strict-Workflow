Time blocks allow you to cycle through pomodors for a set period of time.

You can pick your preferred length of blocks for the modes below. TIP: Timeblocks are great if you want to block a certain block of time to focus on a particular task. During a timeblock session, pomodoro will continue to cycle through work and rest phases. To keep you discipled, there is no way to stop time block.
